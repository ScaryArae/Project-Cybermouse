The largest nomad group operating within the swamps around [[EPCOT]], The Flock is known to have ties to [[The Traveler]] and [[Dynamico Shipping & Transport|Dynamico]]. The flock is the premier force in EPCOT for illicit shipping transportation opportunities, something that has gotten them classified as a highly dangerous gang by EPCOT authorities.
