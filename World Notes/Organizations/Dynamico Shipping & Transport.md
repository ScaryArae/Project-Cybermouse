From their headquarters upon [[The Traveler|the traveler]], Dynamico is a key player in the [[EPCOT]]Corporate Landscape, serving as the SOLE company who handles imports and exports. Nearly 2,000 Dynamico Vehicles pass through the airport and highways of the city each day, and every so often the traveler itself arrives, carrying with it an unimaginable amount of goods.
