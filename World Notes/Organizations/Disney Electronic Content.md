Originally founded in 1995 under the name "Disney Interactive," the company was formed to develop and publish computer and videogame console software. As the age of [the Net](https://cyberpunk.fandom.com/wiki/The_Net "The Net") dawned upon the world, however, Disney Electronic Content quickly rose to become one of [[The Disney Company]]'s most prominent branches.
