IEC Epcot is the local office for the [world's largest manufacturer of cyberware](https://cyberpunk.fandom.com/wiki/International_Electric_Corporation). While they have no local manufacturing plants, instead having their products imported through Dynamico services, this local office acts as a headquarters for distribution and installation services. IEC EPCOT operates dozens of stores and installation clinics across [[EPCOT|the city]].
